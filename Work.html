Visitor Guideline

At Great Ormond Street Hospital (GOSH), we know that visits from family and friends are hugely important to the children and young people we care for. The need to protect people from COVID-19 disrupted these visits for a long time. Since July 2022 we have been able to welcome back friends and family into the hospital, as long as some simple precautions to protect our vulnerable patients from infections are followed.
